**Junior Web Publisher**

We have an exciting A Junior Web Publisher to join our friendly and experienced Digital Team here at Chiswick Park. As Junior Web Developer you will be responsible for the creation and publication of approved content for all QVC digital platforms. You will Support Digital Designers in best practice for creating W3C standard accessible web pages and also ensure their work does not adversely affects the sites performance/scalability. You will work in a skilled, agile, cross-functional team responsible for delivering customer-facing content. On a daily basis you will liaise with the E Commerce, Design and Copy teams, so you will need to be an excellent communicator with the ability to build strong working relationships internally and externally. Prioritisation & Time management is a key part of this role, you will need to ensure work is prioritised according to business needs and time is managed effectively to meet multiple requirement.
To be successful in this role the ideal candidate will:
*Hold a relevant Degree
*Ideally you will have solid experience in working with CMS and publishing regular content (WordPress and AEM experience would be advantageous).
*Prioritising and managing your own work to meet tight deadlines is key in this role.
*Proficient in Web useability, accessibility, W3C protocol’s and cross browser compatibility.
*Basic Front End knowledge is a plus.
